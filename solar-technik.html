Solar-technik
2015

LED strip, solar panels, microcontoller, roofing panels, sunlight.

Installation for PARK15 exhibition, Cannizaro Park, Wimbledon. May 2015.

Solar-technik is an electronic installation that measures and projects a standardised replication of experienced sunlight. This is the ambient sunlight, as experinenced by the viewer, amplified in the fluctuation of one hundred meters of LED strip in a cyclical system of sunlight powering an artifical projection of iteslf.

This is a standardised replication of sensory experience which interprets the same information as is received by the viewer. The digital interpretation of this information, via a sensor, necesarily translates and interprets this information to map the fluctuation in sunlight, too small to be detecteded by the human eye, and provides a technological response to sensory experience.
